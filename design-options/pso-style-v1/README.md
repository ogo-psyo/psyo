# Псё — style directions v1

10 visual directions for the iPhone/PWA/native-wrapper redesign.

1. Vet Card OS — clean medical, trustworthy, warm clinical.
2. Dog Park Map — outdoor/map-first, walks and places.
3. Paw Brutalism — loud anti-cute poster style.
4. Soft Toy App — friendly plush consumer app.
5. Premium Kennel Club — editorial/premium owner journal.
6. Neo Pet OS — modern iOS/App Store product feel.
7. Tamagotchi Companion — digital pet status/retention.
8. Field Notebook — authentic handwritten owner log.
9. Safety Radar — zones/alerts/guardian system.
10. Minimal Calm — quiet premium minimalism.

Recommendation shortlist:
- safest product default: 6 Neo Pet OS
- strongest functional thesis: 2 Dog Park Map or 9 Safety Radar
- warmest mass-market: 4 Soft Toy App
- most distinctive/premium: 5 Premium Kennel Club
- most Ogo/Ruslan-bold: 3 Paw Brutalism
